About Me

Fascinated with video games from a very young age, I felt intrigued to learn the art of creating my own virtual worlds. I started by learning programming and studying the inner workings of different games and their engines in my spare time. Since then I have created small amateur game engine(s) of my own, handful of graphical/gameplay demos, and few mods for games I enjoy playing. I've made my first steps by joining the games industry in 2016 and becoming a games tester for Testronic, two years later joining CD Projekt Red and their in-house development team to work on Cyberpunk 2077.
